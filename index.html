51 students were accepted into Summer of Bitcoin 2021!

Summer of Bitcoin is a global summer internship program focused on introducing student developers to bitcoin open-source development

Students are matched with open source, free software and technology-related organizations working on bitcoin to write code, earn BTC and put their summer break to good use. The organizations provide mentors who help the students onboard to the project and contribute code. The program requires 10-12 weeks of time commitment with a minimum expectation of 30 hours a week and is open to students, age 18 and older enrolled in a post secondary academic program in most countries.

For students

  • Learn about bitcoin
  • Contribute to open source
  • Earn bitcoin
  • Contribute to monetary revolution!

For participating organizations

  • More code and more code reviews
  • Bring in new developers and full-time contributors
  • Support open source development
  • Contribute to monetary revolution!

Adi is a software engineer, product manager and a technological activist. He founded Summer of Bitcoin to help university students learn about and contribute to Bitcoin. He's interested in accelerating the world's transition to a bitcoin standard.

Bitcoin: A Technical Crash Course

A lecture series on how Bitcoin works with Kalle Rosenbaum (author of Grokking Bitcoin)

Bitcoin Wallet Workshop

A workshop on building a bitcoin wallet on Android using BDK with thunderbiscuit

Decentralized Money on a Centralized Internet with Dhruv Mehta

Dhruv joins us to talk about why Bitcoin is important to him and how we are building something decentralized on a centralized infrastructure.

The CS in Bitcoin with Sanket Kanjalkar

Sanket joins us to talk about the multifaceted system that Bitcoin is - an intricate fusion of core CS concepts such as cryptography, distributed systems, networking, algorithms, operating systems, game theory, programming languages, and system security.

Decentralized Open-Source Development with Amiti Uttarwar

Amiti joins us to talk about her understanding of bitcoin, its trust models and how open-source development works in bitcoin core.

Deep Dive Into Bitcoin Mechanics with Matthew Zipkin

Matthew joins us to talk about the design and mechanics of a decentralized, censorship-resistant money - Bitcoin.

Layering to Scale Bitcoin with Will Clark

Will joins us to talk about layering technologies to scale bitcoin, the history and philosophy of one such technology called the Lightning Network and how far we've come with it.
