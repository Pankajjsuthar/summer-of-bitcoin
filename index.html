Summer of Bitcoin
Spend the summer learning Bitcoin and open-source development

About

Summer of Bitcoin is a global program focused on bringing more student developers into Bitcoin open source software development. Students work with a Bitcoin developer on a 10 week programming project.

Weekly Video Calls

Weekly video calls with a mentor to prioritize and further your project goals.

Stipend

All students that complete the program will receive $2,500 for their 10 weeks of participation.

Join Us

Apply to participate in Summer of Bitcoin

We are looking for students interested in learning about open-source development and contributing to Bitcoin Core.

  • Complete the application
  • If you are a good fit for the program, we'll ask you to complete an exercise
  • Once exercise is completed, you'll have a brief call with a representative

James O'Beirne

Carl is a developer at Chaincode Labs where he contributes to rust-bitcoin and Bitcoin Core. He is excited about systems engineering and incentive-compatible uses of permissionless money.
